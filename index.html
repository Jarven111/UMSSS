UMSSS: A Dataset for Visual Scene Semantic Segmentation in Underground Mines

Dataset Introduction

The UMSSS (Underground Mine Scene Semantic Segmentation) dataset is designed to assist research in the semantic segmentation of underground mine environments. This dataset contains a variety of labeled images representing different underground mine scenarios, with detailed annotations for various objects and structures commonly found in mines.
